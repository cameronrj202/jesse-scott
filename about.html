Quick Tips From Jesse

I see a lot of interesting looking hip thrusts 😂 Here’s a few things to think about in order for you to do them correctly, safely and engage the right muscles

✅Spine long - no arching your back or anterior pelvic tilting to compensate for range. Once your glutes are fully contracted theres no need to go any further up. Also, try bring your chest up with your body as your coming down, to maintain neutral spine and avoid overusing your back extensors.

✅Knees inline with your ankle vertically at the top range of the movement - if your feet are too far back towards your body or too far forward, this will take the tension away from your glutes and into other areas. So to keep the glutes isolated, check your feet position

✅Knees pushed out - to get more than just hip extension, push your knees out laterally to incorporate external rotation of the hip. This will activate more muscles so you can work the side of your glutes and not just the back of them 👊🏼
